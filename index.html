Why Water+ Club?

Modern Water, Delivered

Most tap water in the UK is safe to drink, but by the time it reaches your glass it may have travelled through miles of ageing pipes, picking up tastes, odours, and impurities that can affect quality and enjoyment.

At Water+ Club, we believe staying hydrated should be simple, enjoyable, and something you can trust.

Pure Water

Our purified water is carefully filtered to remove unwanted contaminants, delivering:

  • Clean, crisp taste
  • Consistent quality every time
  • No chlorine taste or odour
  • Convenient home or office delivery
  • Reduced reliance on single-use store purchases

Functional Water

Hydration is just the beginning. Our functional water options are enhanced with carefully selected vitamins, minerals, and nutrients designed to support modern lifestyles.

Depending on the blend, benefits may include:

  • Daily hydration support
  • Added electrolytes for active lifestyles
  • Vitamins to complement a balanced diet
  • Convenient nutrition on the go
  • Great taste without sugary soft drinks

Better Hydration, Less Hassle

No carrying heavy bottles home. No last-minute supermarket trips. Just premium water delivered directly to your door, when you need it.

Why not just buy water from the shop?

Water+ Club is being designed around convenience, premium water, and better packaging choices, so customers can avoid repeated shop trips and bulky plastic-heavy restocks.
